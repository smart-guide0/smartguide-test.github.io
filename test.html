    How does Smart Guide work?
    Smart Guide utilizes a depth camera mounted on ​sunglasses to scan your surroundings.
     This information ​is then processed by a microcontroller and relayed to ​discreet vibrating bracelets worn on your limbs.
      The ​vibrations provide clear and intuitive guidance, helping ​you navigate obstacles, identify objects like currency ​and products,
       and even recognize faces.
            
The speaker describes everything the sunglasses detect (people , obstacles ,products)
        
    The Hand and leg bands emit vibrations that help the user to move his hands and legs in the proper directions
        to move , grabs things etc.
